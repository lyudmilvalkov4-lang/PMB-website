Manufacturing

PMB plant engineered for controlled polymer modification.

Polysan's manufacturing system is organized around heated storage, accurate weighing, high-shear milling, controlled circulation, and repeatable production of polymer modified asphalt binders.

PMB Plant

modiFALT20 PMB plant platform.

The plant is designed for polymer modified bitumen production with an integrated control cabin, PLC and computer SCADA controlled operation, semi-automatic backup capability, and containerized construction for practical installation.

Up to 20 t/hProduction capacity with SBS polymer according to manufacturer procedures
Up to 6 tBatch capacity through weighing and mixing tanks

Systems

Core equipment in the PMB manufacturing line.

01

PMB Plant

Containerized PMB production unit with integrated control cabin, automated process control, high-shear milling, polymer feed, and bitumen transfer systems.

02

Tanks

Two weighing and mixing tanks support accurate bitumen weighing, strong agitation, heated jackets, thermal insulation, inspection access, and polymer dispersion.

03

Thermal Oil System

Hot-oil jacketed piping, valves, mill body, pumps, and tank jackets maintain process temperature and reduce polymer adhesion in dead zones.

04

Control & Safety

Digital weight indicators, temperature monitoring, protected electrical panels, pneumatic valves, and operator controls support reliable production and maintenance.
