Product Specification

PG 70-22

Specification limits and batch-result format for PG 70-22 paving grade binder.

Applications

Where PG 70-22 Fits

  • Higher-traffic municipal and state routes
  • Intersections, ramps, and slow-moving traffic areas
  • Commercial and industrial paving with heavier loading
  • Mix designs requiring improved high-temperature stiffness
Performance Benefits

Stronger High-Temperature Resistance

  • Improved rutting resistance versus standard binder grades
  • Better support for heavy tire loads and braking zones
  • Controlled viscosity for plant handling
  • Certified laboratory batch analysis support
