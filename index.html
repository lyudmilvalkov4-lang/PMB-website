About Us

A Baltimore producer backed by international petroleum-products expertise.

Polysan is a subsidiary of Lotus Oil Trade, a Bulgarian company and the parent company of Polysan. The company was formed to bring focused PMB production, technical binder knowledge, and terminal-based asphalt supply to the U.S. market.

Our mission is to help customers build longer-lasting pavement by combining production discipline, certified laboratory analysis, and practical logistics support from the Origin Baltimore Terminals location.
