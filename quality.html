Certified laboratory control for every batch.

Every Polysan production batch is analyzed through a certified laboratory, with testing support including DSR, BBR, PAV, and RTFO equipment.

Certified Laboratory

Every batch moves through a defined test sequence.

Each method verifies a specific part of binder performance before shipment documentation is issued.

01

DSR

Dynamic Shear Rheometer

DSR testing measures rheological behavior at high and intermediate service temperatures. It is used for original binder, RTFO-aged binder, and PAV-aged residue checks such as G*/sin delta and G*.sin delta.

02

BBR

Bending Beam Rheometer

BBR testing evaluates low-temperature stiffness and creep response. The S value and m-value help confirm that the binder meets cold-weather performance requirements.

03

PAV

Pressure Aging Vessel

PAV conditioning simulates long-term binder aging in service. The aged residue is then tested to verify retained performance after extended pavement exposure.

04

RTFO

Rolling Thin-Film Oven

RTFO testing simulates short-term aging during plant mixing and placement. The residual binder is checked for mass change and high-temperature shear performance.
